EKVA CARE

Dietetics - The Science of Being Healthy!

At EKVA Care, we believe that true wellness comes from embracing a balanced lifestyle, not just counting calories. Founded by registered dietitian Ekaterina Vasileva, our mission is to educate and inspire you to nourish your body with wholesome foods, sustainable habits, and a positive mindset. Join us in celebrating the art of being healthy, where every step towards wellness is a victory.

With our guidance, you'll discover the joy of eating well and living fully. Let's embark on this journey to health together, focusing on vitality, balance, and the pure pleasure of nourishing your body.

Registered dietitian

EKATERINA VASILEVA

It's not important to be skinny, let's be healthy!

Ekaterina Vasileva is a registered dietitian (HCPC) and certified personal trainer. She is dedicated to providing evidence-based nutrition strategies that help people with various health conditions manage symptoms, improve overall well-being, and enhance their quality of life.

Ekaterina earned her Bachelor’s degree in Clinical Dietetics from Queen Margaret University in Scotland, where she researched the Mediterranean diet’s impact on cardiovascular disease risk. She gained extensive clinical experience during a seven-month internship in Scottish hospitals, working in fields such as oncology, rehabilitation, weight management, geriatrics, paediatrics and diabetes care.

Currently, Ekaterina practices in England as part of a multidisciplinary rehabilitation team, supporting patients recovering from post-traumatic injuries. She is also an expert on the Womeno team, helping women navigate menopause through tailored nutritional guidance. Additionally, she is expanding her expertise in fertility nutrition, supporting couples on their journey to fertility optimisation and helping them navigate infertility challenges with evidence-based dietary strategies.
