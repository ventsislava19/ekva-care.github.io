Frequently Asked Questions

Booking a consultation is quick and easy!

  1. Choose an available date and time using the calendar above.

Following the initial consultation, a comprehensive dietetic assessment and detailed report can be provided if further guidance is required. This report includes a full dietary analysis of your current nutritional habits, alongside a clear summary of your health goals. It offers personalised nutrition recommendations tailored to your individual needs, highlighting key areas for improvement and providing expert advice to support your long-term health and well-being.

  • Detailed Health Assessment: Discussion of your medical history, lifestyle, dietary habits, specific health concerns, medication and supplement use.
  • Nutritional Goal Setting: Collaborative development of personalised health goals, considering your unique needs.
  • Dietary Analysis: Review of your current eating patterns and identification of potential improvements or gaps.
  • Lifestyle and Behavioral Insights: Exploration of factors such as stress, sleep, and physical activity that affect your nutrition and health.
  • Personalised Action Plan: Development of a tailored plan with practical recommendations to support your long-term health and well-being.
  • Q&A Session: Opportunity for you to ask questions and clarify any doubts regarding your diet and lifestyle.
  • Review of Your Progress: We’ll discuss how you’ve been getting on with your action plan and any challenges you’ve faced along the way.
  • Assessing Your Health Outcomes: Together, we’ll look at any improvements or changes in your health, such as energy levels, weight, or other markers.
  • Adjusting Your Plan: Based on your feedback and progress, we’ll make any necessary adjustments to your nutrition plan to keep you on track.
  • Further Nutritional Guidance: I’ll provide more advice on areas where extra education/support is needed.
  • Addressing New Concerns: If any new health issues or questions have come up since our last session, we’ll talk through them.
  • Motivational Support: I’ll offer encouragement and practical tips to help you maintain your new healthy habits in the long term.
  • Setting New Goals when required: We’ll refine your goals to ensure they stay aligned with your evolving needs and keep you moving towards your desired outcomes.

To make the most of your consultation, please have the following ready:

  • - Recent Blood Test Results – If you have any recent blood work (ideally from the past 3–6 months), please have it available to share. This helps us better understand your health status.
  • - 3-Day Food Diary – Track everything you eat and drink for three days, including portion sizes and ingredients. Be as detailed as possible, noting exact foods and beverages consumed, portion sizes and meal times.

Our consultations are conducted online for your convenience. You can choose one of the following platforms, while booking your appointment: Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Viber, WhatsApp.

After booking your appointment, you’ll receive a confirmation email with a link to join the call on your preferred platform.

The number of sessions varies depending on your goals and needs. Some clients see results in just a few sessions, while others prefer ongoing support for long-term changes. We can discuss this during your initial appointment.

Q: When do I need to pay for my appointment?
A: Payments for appointments must be made no later than 24 hours before the scheduled consultation. We recommend making payment as early as possible to secure your spot. If payment is not received by this deadline, the consultation will be canceled automatically.

Q: Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?
A: Yes - you can, no later than 24 hours before the booked appointment. To edit the status of your booking, please use the link in the confirmation email, which you will recieve after a booking is made.

Q: What happens if I miss my appointment or cancel after the 24-hour deadline?
A: No-shows or cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance are not eligible for a refund and cannot be rescheduled.

Q: Do you offer refunds after the consultation?
A: No. Refunds are not provided for completed services under any circumstances.
